Submit your talk for WebCamp Zagreb 2015.

  • Conference takes place on Saturday, 4th October 2014 in Zagreb.
  • All talks are 25 minutes, including 5 minutes for Q&A.
  • Prior experience is not a prerequisite, we welcome first timers.
  • Talks may be in English or Croatian, but we’d be extra happy if you choose to speak in English.
  • Multiple talk submissions are welcome.
  • First 30 submissions get free tickets.

For more details see the conference web site http://2015.webcampzg.org.
